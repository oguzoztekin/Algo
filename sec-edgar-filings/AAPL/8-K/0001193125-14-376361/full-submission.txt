&#147;With amazing innovations in our new iPhones, iPads and Macs, as well as iOS 8 and OS X Yosemite, we are heading into the holidays with Apple&#146;s strongest product lineup ever. We are also incredibly excited about Apple Watch and other great

from operations in the September quarter,&#148; said Luca Maestri, Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;We continued to execute aggressively against our capital return program, spending over $20 billion in the quarter and bringing cumulative returns to $94
billion.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">Apple is providing the following guidance for its fiscal 2015 first quarter: </P>
