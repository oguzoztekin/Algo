 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="3"><B>Apple Reports Record Results </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>47.8 Million iPhones Sold; 22.9 Million iPads Sold
</B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:0px; "><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;January 23, 2013&#151;Apple<FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP
STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today announced financial results for its 13-week fiscal 2013 first quarter ended December&nbsp;29, 2012. The Company posted record quarterly revenue of $54.5 billion
and record quarterly net profit of $13.1 billion, or $13.81 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $46.3 billion and net profit of $13.1 billion, or $13.87 per diluted share, in the 14-week year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 38.6
percent compared to 44.7 percent in the year-ago quarter. International sales accounted for 61 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Average
weekly revenue was $4.2 billion in the quarter compared to $3.3 billion in the year-ago quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Company sold a record 47.8&nbsp;million
iPhones in the quarter, compared to 37&nbsp;million in the year-ago quarter. Apple also sold a record 22.9&nbsp;million iPads during the quarter, compared to 15.4&nbsp;million in the year-ago quarter. The Company sold 4.1&nbsp;million Macs, compared
to 5.2&nbsp;million in the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 12.7&nbsp;million iPods in the quarter, compared to 15.4&nbsp;million in the year-ago quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple&#146;s Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend of $2.65 per share of the Company&#146;s common stock. The dividend is payable on February&nbsp;14, 2013, to shareholders of record as of the
close of business on February&nbsp;11, 2013. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re thrilled with record revenue of over $54 billion and sales of over
75&nbsp;million iOS devices in a single quarter,&#148; said Tim Cook, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;We&#146;re very confident in our product pipeline as we continue to focus on innovation and making the best products in the world.&#148; </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re pleased to have generated over $23 billion in cash flow from operations during the quarter,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s
