 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="3"><B>Apple Reports Third Quarter Results </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>All-Time Record Revenue and Earnings
</B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>iPhone Sales Grow 142 Percent; iPad Sales Grow 183 Percent </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:0px;"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;July 19, 2011&#151;Apple<FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP
STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today announced financial results for its fiscal 2011 third quarter ended June&nbsp;25, 2011. The Company posted record quarterly revenue of $28.57 billion and record
quarterly net profit of $7.31 billion, or $7.79 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $15.70 billion and net quarterly profit of $3.25 billion, or $3.51 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 41.7 percent
compared to 39.1 percent in the year-ago quarter. International sales accounted for 62 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Company
sold 20.34&nbsp;million iPhones in the quarter, representing 142 percent unit growth over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 9.25&nbsp;million iPads during the quarter, a 183 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. The Company sold
3.95&nbsp;million Macs during the quarter, a 14 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 7.54&nbsp;million iPods, a 20 percent unit decline from the year-ago quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re thrilled to deliver our best quarter ever, with revenue up 82 percent and profits up 125 percent,&#148; said Steve Jobs, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;Right now, we&#146;re very focused and
excited about bringing iOS 5 and iCloud to our users this fall.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We are extremely pleased with our performance which drove
quarterly cash flow from operations of $11.1 billion, an increase of 131 percent year-over-year,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;Looking ahead to the fourth fiscal quarter of 2011, we expect revenue of about $25 billion and we
expect diluted earnings per share of about $5.50.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple will provide live streaming of its Q3 2011 financial results conference call
beginning at 2:00 p.m. PDT on July&nbsp;19, 2011 at <U>www.apple.com/quicktime/qtv/earningsq311</U>. This webcast will also be available for replay for approximately two weeks thereafter. </FONT></P>
