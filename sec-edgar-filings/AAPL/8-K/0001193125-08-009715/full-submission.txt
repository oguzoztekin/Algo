STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:3px;line-Height:95%; vertical-align:top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;January 22, 2008&#151;Apple<FONT FACE="Times New Roman"
SIZE="1"><SUP>&reg;</SUP></FONT> today announced financial results for its fiscal 2008 first quarter ended December&nbsp;29, 2007. The Company posted revenue of $9.6 billion and net quarterly profit of $1.58 billion, or $1.76 per diluted share.
These results compare to revenue of $7.1 billion and net quarterly profit of $1 billion, or $1.14 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 34.7 percent, up from 31.2 percent in the year-ago quarter. International sales accounted
for 45 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:3px;line-Height:95%; vertical-align:top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple shipped 2,319,000 Macintosh<FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP>&reg;</SUP></FONT> computers, representing 44 percent unit growth and 47 percent revenue growth over the year-ago quarter. The Company sold 22,121,000 iPods during the quarter, representing five percent unit
growth and 17 percent revenue growth over the year-ago quarter. Quarterly iPhone&#153; sales were 2,315,000. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re thrilled to report our best
quarter ever, with the highest revenue and earnings in Apple&#146;s history,&#148; said Steve Jobs, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;We have an incredibly strong new product pipeline for 2008, starting with MacBook Air, Mac Pro and iTunes Movie Rentals in
the first two weeks.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;Apple&#146;s revenue grew 35 percent year-over-year to $9.6 billion, an increase of almost $2.5 billion over the previous
December quarter&#146;s record-breaking results,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;Our strong results produced cash flow from operations of over $2.7 billion during the quarter, yielding an ending cash balance of over $18.4
billion. Looking ahead to the second quarter of fiscal 2008, we expect revenue of about $6.8 billion and earnings per diluted share of about $.94.&#148; </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:3px;line-Height:95%; vertical-align:top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple will provide live streaming of its Q1 2008 financial results conference call utilizing QuickTime<FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP>&reg;</SUP></FONT>, Apple&#146;s standards-based technology for live and on-demand audio and video streaming. The live webcast will begin at 2:00 p.m. PST on Tuesday, January&nbsp;22, 2008 at
www.apple.com/quicktime/qtv/earningsq108/ and will also be available for replay. </FONT></P>
