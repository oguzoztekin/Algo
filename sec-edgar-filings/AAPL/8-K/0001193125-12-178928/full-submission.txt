 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="3"><B>Apple Reports Second Quarter Results </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Record March Quarter Sales of iPhones, iPads and
Macs </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Net Profit Increases 94% Year-over-Year </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:0px;"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;April 24, 2012&#151;Apple<FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today
announced financial results for its fiscal 2012 second quarter ended March&nbsp;31, 2012. The Company posted quarterly revenue of $39.2 billion and quarterly net profit of $11.6 billion, or $12.30 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue
of $24.7 billion and net profit of $6.0 billion, or $6.40 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 47.4 percent compared to 41.4 percent in the year-ago quarter. International sales accounted for 64 percent of the quarter&#146;s
revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Company sold 35.1&nbsp;million iPhones in the quarter, representing 88 percent unit growth over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold
11.8&nbsp;million iPads during the quarter, a 151 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. The Company sold 4&nbsp;million Macs during the quarter, a 7 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 7.7&nbsp;million iPods, a 15
percent unit decline from the year-ago quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re thrilled with sales of over 35&nbsp;million iPhones and almost
12&nbsp;million iPads in the March quarter,&#148; said Tim&nbsp;Cook, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;The new iPad&nbsp;is off to a great start, and across the year you&#146;re going to see a lot more of the kind of innovation that only Apple can
deliver.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;Our record March quarter results drove $14 billion in cash flow from operations,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer,
Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;Looking ahead to the third fiscal quarter, we expect revenue of about $34 billion and diluted earnings per share of about $8.68.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
