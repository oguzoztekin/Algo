 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="3"><B>Apple Reports First Quarter Results </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Highest Quarterly Revenue and Earnings Ever
</B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>All-Time Record iPhone, iPad and Mac Sales </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:0px;"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;January 24, 2012&#151;Apple<FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today
announced financial results for its fiscal 2012 first quarter which spanned 14 weeks and ended December&nbsp;31, 2011. The Company posted record quarterly revenue of $46.33 billion and record quarterly net profit of $13.06 billion, or $13.87 per
diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $26.74 billion and net quarterly profit of $6 billion, or $6.43 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 44.7 percent compared to 38.5 percent in the year-ago quarter.
International sales accounted for 58 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Company sold 37.04&nbsp;million iPhones in the quarter,
representing 128 percent unit growth over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 15.43&nbsp;million iPads during the quarter, a 111 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. The Company sold 5.2&nbsp;million Macs during the quarter, a 26 percent
unit increase over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold 15.4&nbsp;million iPods, a 21 percent unit decline from the year-ago quarter. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re thrilled with our outstanding results and record-breaking sales of iPhones, iPads and Macs,&#148; said Tim Cook, Apple&#146;s CEO.
&#147;Apple&#146;s momentum is incredibly strong, and we have some amazing new products in the pipeline.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We are very happy to
have generated over $17.5 billion in cash flow from operations during the December quarter,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;Looking ahead to the second fiscal quarter of 2012, which will span 13 weeks, we expect revenue of
about $32.5 billion and we expect diluted earnings per share of about $8.50.&#148; </FONT></P>
