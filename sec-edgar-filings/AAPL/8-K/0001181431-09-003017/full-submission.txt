<P align="left">
<FONT face="serif">Apple CEO Steve Jobs today sent the following email to all Apple employees:</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
<TABLE><TR><TD nowrap>
<FONT face="serif">Team,</FONT><BR>
</TD></TR></TABLE>
</P>
<P align="left">
<FONT face="serif">I am sure all of you saw my letter last week sharing something very personal with the Apple community. Unfortunately, the curiosity over my personal health continues to be a distraction not only for me and my family, but everyone
else at Apple as well. In addition, during the past week I have learned that my health-related issues are more complex than I originally thought.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
<FONT face="serif">In order to take myself out of the limelight and focus on my health, and to allow everyone at Apple to focus on delivering extraordinary products, I have decided to take a medical leave of absence until the end of June.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
<FONT face="serif">I have asked Tim Cook to be responsible for Apple&#146;s day to day operations, and I know he and the rest of the executive management team will do a great job. As CEO, I plan to remain involved in major strategic decisions while
I am out. Our board of directors fully supports this plan.</FONT></P>
<P align="left">
<TABLE><TR><TD nowrap>
<FONT face="serif">I look forward to seeing all of you this summer.</FONT><BR>
</TD></TR></TABLE>
</P>
<P align="left">
<TABLE><TR><TD nowrap>
<FONT face="serif">Steve</FONT><BR>
