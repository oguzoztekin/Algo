<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Other Events. </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">On November&nbsp;4, 2014, Apple Inc. (&#147;Apple&#148;) entered into an underwriting agreement (the
&#147;Underwriting Agreement&#148;) with Goldman, Sachs&nbsp;&amp; Co. as representative of the several underwriters named therein, for the issuance and sale by Apple of <FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman">&#128;</FONT>1,400,000,000 aggregate
principal amount of Apple&#146;s 1.000% Notes due 2022 (the &#147;2022 Notes&#148;), and <FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman">&#128;</FONT>1,400,000,000 aggregate principal amount of Apple&#146;s 1.625% Notes due 2026 (the &#147;2026
Notes&#148; and together with the 2022 Notes, the &#147;Notes&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">The Notes will be issued pursuant to an indenture, dated as of April&nbsp;29, 2013 (the
&#147;Indenture&#148;), between Apple and The Bank of New York Mellon Trust Company, N.A., as trustee (the &#147;Trustee&#148;), together with the officer&#146;s certificate dated as of November&nbsp;10, 2014 issued pursuant thereto establishing the
terms of each series of the Notes (the &#147;Officer&#146;s Certificate&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">The Notes are being issued pursuant to Apple&#146;s Registration Statement on Form
S-3 filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April&nbsp;29, 2013 (Reg. No.&nbsp;333-188191) (the &#147;Registration Statement&#148;). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">Interest on the
Notes will be payable annually on November&nbsp;10 of each year, beginning on November&nbsp;10, 2015, and on the applicable maturity date for each series of Notes. The 2022 Notes will mature on November&nbsp;10, 2022 and the 2026 Notes will mature
on November&nbsp;10, 2026. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">The Notes will be Apple&#146;s senior unsecured obligations and will rank equally with Apple&#146;s other unsecured and unsubordinated
debt from time to time outstanding. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL">The foregoing descriptions of the Underwriting Agreement, the Indenture and the Officer&#146;s Certificate (including the forms
of the Notes) are qualified in their entirety by the terms of such agreements and documents. The Underwriting Agreement and the Officer&#146;s Certificate (including the forms of the Notes) are attached hereto as Exhibits 1.1 and 4.1 through 4.3,
respectively, and incorporated herein by reference. The Indenture is filed as Exhibit 4.1 to the Registration Statement. </P>
