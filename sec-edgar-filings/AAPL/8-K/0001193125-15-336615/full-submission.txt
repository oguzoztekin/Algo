Board. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify">As a non-employee director, Mr.&nbsp;Bell receives a $100,000 annual retainer for his service on the Board, paid in quarterly installments,

December&nbsp;28, 2013. Upon his appointment, Mr.&nbsp;Bell received an automatic initial grant of 1,007 restricted stock units under the Director Plan. In connection with his appointment, Apple and Mr.&nbsp;Bell will enter into Apple&#146;s
standard indemnification agreement for directors, the form of which was filed with the SEC on July&nbsp;22,&nbsp;2009 as Exhibit 10.2 to Apple&#146;s Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarterly period ended June&nbsp;27, 2009. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:9pt; font-family:ARIAL" ALIGN="justify">There are no transactions between Apple and Mr.&nbsp;Bell that would be required to be reported under Item&nbsp;404(a) of Regulation S-K. </P>
