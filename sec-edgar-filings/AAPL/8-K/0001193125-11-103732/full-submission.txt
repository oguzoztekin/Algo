 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="3"><B>Apple Reports Second Quarter Results </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Record March Quarter Drives 83 Percent Revenue
Growth, 95 Percent Profit Growth </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Record iPhone Sales Grow 113 Percent </B></FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6px;margin-bottom:0px;padding-bottom:0px;"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">CUPERTINO, California&#151;April 20, 2011&#151;Apple<FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="1"><SUP
STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today announced financial results for its fiscal 2011 second quarter ended March&nbsp;26, 2011. The Company posted record second quarter revenue of $24.67 billion and
record second quarter net profit of $5.99 billion, or $6.40 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $13.50 billion and net quarterly profit of $3.07 billion, or $3.33 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 41.4
percent compared to 41.7 percent in the year-ago quarter. International sales accounted for 59 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple
sold 3.76&nbsp;million Macs during the quarter, a 28 percent unit increase over the year-ago quarter. The Company sold 18.65&nbsp;million iPhones in the quarter, representing 113 percent unit growth over the year-ago quarter. Apple sold
9.02&nbsp;million iPods during the quarter, representing a 17 percent unit decline from the year-ago quarter. The Company also sold 4.69&nbsp;million iPads during the quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;With quarterly revenue growth of 83 percent and profit growth of 95 percent, we&#146;re firing on all cylinders,&#148; said Steve Jobs, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;We will continue to innovate on all
fronts throughout the remainder of the year.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We are extremely pleased with our record March quarter revenue and earnings and cash
flow from operations of over $6.2 billion,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO. &#147;Looking ahead to the third fiscal quarter of 2011, we expect revenue of about $23 billion and we expect diluted earnings per share of about $5.03.&#148;
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple will provide live streaming of its Q2 2011 financial results conference call beginning at 2:00 p.m. PDT on April&nbsp;20, 2011 at
www.apple.com/quicktime/qtv/earningsq211. This webcast will also be available for replay for approximately two weeks thereafter. </FONT></P>
