STYLE="vertical-align:baseline; position:relative; bottom:.8ex">&reg;</SUP></FONT> today announced financial results for its fiscal 2013 third quarter ended June&nbsp;29, 2013. The Company posted quarterly revenue of $35.3 billion and quarterly net
profit of $6.9 billion, or $7.47 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $35 billion and net profit of $8.8 billion, or $9.32 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 36.9 percent compared to 42.8 percent in the
year-ago quarter. International sales accounted for 57 percent of the quarter&#146;s revenue. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Company sold 31.2&nbsp;million iPhones, a

4&nbsp;million in the year-ago quarter. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple&#146;s Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend of $3.05 per share of the Company&#146;s
common stock. The dividend is payable on August&nbsp;15, 2013, to shareholders of record as of the close of business on August&nbsp;12, 2013. </FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We are especially proud of our record June quarter iPhone sales of over 31&nbsp;million and the strong growth in revenue from iTunes, Software and
Services,&#148; said Tim Cook, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;We are really excited about the upcoming releases of iOS 7 and OS X Mavericks, and we are laser-focused and working hard on some amazing new products that we will introduce in the fall and
across 2014.&#148; </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We generated $7.8 billion in cash flow from operations during the quarter and are pleased to have returned $18.8
billion in cash to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT STYLE="font-family:Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Apple is providing
the following guidance for its fiscal 2013 fourth quarter: </FONT></P> <P STYLE="font-size:6px;margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px">&nbsp;</P>
