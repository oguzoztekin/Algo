$1.07 billion, or $1.19 per diluted share. These results compare to revenue of $5.41 billion and net quarterly profit of $818 million, or $.92 per diluted share, in the year-ago quarter. Gross margin was 34.8 percent, down from 36.9 percent in the

during the quarter, representing 12 percent unit growth and seven percent revenue growth over the year-ago quarter. Quarterly iPhone&#153; units sold were 717,000 compared to 270,000 in the year-ago-quarter. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:12px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re proud to report the best June quarter for both revenue and earnings in Apple&#146;s history,&#148; said Steve Jobs, Apple&#146;s CEO. &#147;We set a new

FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;We&#146;re extremely pleased with the growth of our business and the generation of almost $5.4 billion in cash in the first three quarters of fiscal 2008,&#148; said Peter Oppenheimer, Apple&#146;s CFO.
&#147;Looking ahead to the fourth quarter of fiscal 2008, we expect revenue of about $7.8 billion and earnings per diluted share of about $1.00.&#148; </FONT></P> <P
